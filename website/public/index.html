How it works

A teleprompter for closers

Scout runs silently on your Mac while you're on a call. You see the overlay — your prospect doesn't.

01

Open Scout, start your call

Launch the app, hit Start. Scout begins listening to both sides of the conversation through your mic and system audio.

02

Scout reads the conversation

Real-time transcription of both speakers. Scout detects the call stage, prospect objections, and what's been covered.

03

Your next line appears

A transparent overlay shows you exactly what to say — personalized to what your prospect just said. One prompt at a time, always relevant.

04

Deliver it. Move forward.

Say the line. Scout detects delivery and immediately prepares the next prompt. No manual clicking, no interruptions.

Features

Built for high-ticket closers

🎯

Stage-aware coaching

Scout knows whether you're in introduction, discovery, pitch, or close — and gives you the right prompt for each stage.

🛡️

Objection frameworks

When a prospect raises an objection, Scout immediately surfaces the right identity-shifting phase — Money, Spouse, Think About It, No Time.

🔇

Never interrupts

Scout waits for you to finish speaking before showing a new prompt. No pop-ups mid-sentence. No distractions while you're in flow.

🪟

Click-through overlay

The overlay sits on top of your screen but doesn't block anything — your Zoom, your CRM, your notes. It's invisible to your prospect.

📊

Call analytics

After every call, see which objections came up, how they were handled, what stages you hit, and where opportunities were left on the table.

⚡

Zero setup

Install the app, allow mic and screen recording — that's it. No virtual audio drivers, no complicated config, no API keys to manage.
