Getting started

1
Open the .dmg — drag Scout to your Applications folder.
2
Launch Scout — on first open, right-click → Open if macOS shows a security warning.
3
Grant permissions — allow Microphone and Screen Recording when prompted. Both are required to hear the call.
4
Start a session — select your mic, click Start, and Scout will appear as a transparent bar at the top of your screen.
System requirements: macOS Monterey 12.0 or later · 8 GB RAM or more · Active internet connection for AI coaching.
